Arbitration & Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)

Arbitration and Alternative dispute resolution has expanded over the last several years and has become an important step in the dispute resolution process. The firm has a robust commercial litigation practice with knowledge base in all forms of dispute resolution. Endeavour is made to ensure that disputes are resolved efficiently and cost effectively. Our firm has vast experience in handling arbitration matters and other forms of Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) such as Mediation and Conciliation.

Our firm has experience and expertise in handling complex arbitrations involving multi jurisdictions before various arbitral institutions of India. We have a dedicated team of lawyers with specific expertise to meet our client’s requirements and handle complex arbitration matters. The firm has in-depth understanding and experience in Arbitration / alternate dispute resolution mechanism which can be a valuable alternative to litigation.

Services provided by our firm include:

  • Conducting domestic arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
  • Rendering mediation services to clients.
  • Representing clients before high courts and supreme court in arbitration petitions
  • Enforcing and challenging enforcement of arbitral awards
  • Advising clients on the appropriate venue of arbitration and choice of procedural and substantive law
  • Conducting Lok Adalats with the concerned legal services authorities.
  • Appointment of arbitrator through High Court of Delhi and Supreme Court Of India
  • Representing clients before arbitral tribunals with regard to disputes arising out of agreements containing arbitration clause
  • Applying to stay proceedings brought in breach of arbitration clauses
  • Advising clients on conciliation and mediation matters.
  • Advising clients on alternative dispute resolution laws and procedures
