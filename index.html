Insight to The Team

Mr. Rishi Sethi
Founding member/ Partner
Mr. Sethi is an industrious legal professional who always works diligentlyand thoroughly in order to ensure the beneficial interests of his Clients. He has made an immense impact with an international reputation as an expert and authority on Banking and Commercial laws.
After successfully completing his legal education, he enrolled himself with the Delhi Bar Council in the Year 1999 and started his practice as an Advocate. He expanded his law practice by rigorously assisting the Hon'ble Delhi High Court, NCDRC (National Consumer Dispute Redressal Commission), Debt Recovery Tribunals, Board for Industrial & Financial Reconstruction (BIFR), Monopolies & Restrictive Trade Practices (MRTP) Commission, Central Administrative Tribunal, National Green Tribunal, Arbitration Tribunals and various other Forums. Mr. Sethi is also an empanelled Arbitrator with Delhi International Arbitration Centre at Delhi High Court.
